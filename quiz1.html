About Blitar

Blitar, city and kabupaten (regency), Jawa Timur propinsi (East Java province), Java, Indonesia. It is located 70 miles (113 km) southwest of Surabaya, the provincial capital. The city lies at an elevation of 528 feet (161 m) above sea level. Linked by road and railway with Malang to the east and with Tulungagung to the west, it has a rural character because of the ancient temples and historical sites located in and around the city..
